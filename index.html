Arc Halls

Arc Halls is home to students primarily studying at SLU Maryheights Campus, our neighbour.

Students are separated into male and female floors.

Students can choose between single, double or triple occupancy per room.

Charges include priority access to WiFi (PLDT Fibr), gas and electricity.

Each room has a separate CR and shower rooms, with water heater.
